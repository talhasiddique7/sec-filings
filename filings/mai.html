General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Individual-level registration for natural persons (specific employees) associated with a municipal advisory firm.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: Before the individual engages in municipal advisory activities.
  • Who: Individual municipal advisor representatives.
