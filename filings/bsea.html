General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Form BSEA is an application for registration of security based swap dealers and major.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: This is generally an event-based filing. It must be filed within 4 business days.
  • Who: It must be filed by public entities.

Purpose of BSEA

Copy to Jack Habert, 12/28/201 FORM SBSE-A INSTRUCTIONS A. GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS 1. FORM - Form SBSE-A is the Application for Registration as either a Security-based Swap Dealer or Major Security-based Swap Participant (collectively, “SBS Entities”) by an entity that is not registered or registering with the Commission as a broker-dealer but is registered or registering with the Commodity Futures Trading Commission (“CFTC”) as a swap dealer or major swap participant. These SBS Entities must file this form and a legible copy of the Form 7-R they file with the CFTC (or its designee) to register with the Securities and Exchange Commission. An applicant must also file Schedules A, B, D and F, as appropriate. There is no Schedule E. An entity that is registered or registering with the Commission as a broker-dealer and also is registered or registering with the Commodity Futures Trading Commission (“CFTC”) as a swap dealer or major swap participant should file Form SBSE-BD to register with the Commission as an SBS Entity. 2. Part II Reference

Core Financial Statements

Based on SEC mandates, this form requires the following audited or unaudited financial comparisons:

Balance Sheet

A statement of the assets, liabilities, and capital of a business at a particular point in time, detailing the balance of income and expenditure over the preceding period.

Required disclosure per Item 8 or Regulation S-X.

Income Statement

A financial statement that reports a company's financial performance over a specific accounting period. Also known as Profit and Loss (P&L).

Required disclosure per Item 8 or Regulation S-X.

Cash Flow Statement

A statement that provides aggregate data regarding all cash inflows a company receives from its ongoing operations and external investment sources.

Required disclosure per Item 8 or Regulation S-X.
