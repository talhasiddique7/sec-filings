General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Initial notification filing to register as an investment company under the Investment Company Act of 1940.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: Before beginning operations as a registered investment company.
  • Who: New investment companies registering under the Investment Company Act.

Purpose of N-8A

You may not send a completed printout of this form to the SEC to satisfy a filing obligation.
