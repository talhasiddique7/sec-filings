General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Form PILOT is an initial operation report amendment to initial operation report and qua.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: This is generally an event-based filing. It must be filed within 4 business days.
  • Who: It must be filed by public entities.

GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS 1. Form PILOT is the form a self-regulatory organization (“SRO”) files to notify the Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC” or “Commission” ) of its intention to operate a pilot trading system pursuant to Rule 19b-5, § 240.19b-5, under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (“Exchange Act”). 2.
