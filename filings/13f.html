SEC Filing

13F Series (includes 13F-NT, 13F-E)

KF Notice – No Holdings
Frequency Insider/Ownership
Deadline Within 45 days after each calendar quarter-end (February 14, May 15, August 14, November 14).
Who Files Investment managers exercising investment discretion over $100M+ in US equity securities.
Authority SEC Mandate

Page Contents

General Information Cover Page Information Table Signatures

General Information & Instructions

List

List of Holdings

Detailed table of all Section 13(f) securities (US-listed stocks) held at the end of the quarter.

Shares

Share Count

Reports the total number of shares held and the market value as of the last trading day.

Authority

Investment Discretion

Indicates whether the manager has sole, shared, or no investment discretion.

Voting

Voting Authority

Discloses how much voting power the manager has over corporate actions.

REPORTING ENTITY

Cover & Summary Pages

Cover Page

Identifies the institutional investment manager filing the report, including contact details and reporting period.

Summary Page

Provides the total number of items on the information table, the total value of all holdings, and any other managers included in the report.

PORTFOLIO DATA

Information Table

The core of the 13F filing. It contains a detailed XML table (formerly text) of all reportable US equity holdings.

Issuer

Name of Issuer & Title of Class

The company name and specific class of security (e.g., Common Stock, Put Option, Call Option).

CUSIP

CUSIP Number

The unique nine-character identifier for the security.

Value

Value & Number of Shares

The total fair market value of the position in thousands of dollars, and the exact count of shares (or principal amount) held on the last day of the quarter.

Discretion

Investment Discretion & Voting Authority

Indicates whether the manager has sole, shared, or no voting authority over the shares.

Signatures

The report must be signed by the registrant and by its authorized officers/directors in accordance with SEC rules.
