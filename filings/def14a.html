General Information & Instructions

Filing Summary

  • What: The final official proxy statement sent to shareholders before annual/special meetings. Contains: director nominations, executive compensation tables (CEO/CFO total comp), Say-on-Pay vote, auditor ratification, shareholder proposals, related-party transactions, and equity plan approvals.
  • Why: The definitive source for executive pay, board governance, and shareholder voting. CEO compensation data on Simply Wall St, MarketBeat governance scores, and ISS/Glass Lewis proxy recommendations all derive from DEF 14A. Must-read for ESG analysis and governance risk assessment.
  • When: Mailed or made available at least 40 calendar days before the annual meeting.
  • Who: All public companies holding shareholder meetings.

Related Forms & Specialized Variants

DEFA14A

Additional Definitive Proxy Materials

Supplemental proxy materials distributed after the definitive proxy. Used to respond to activist criticism, ISS/Glass Lewis negative recommendations, or provide additional color on proposals.

DEFC14A

Definitive Proxy ΓÇô Contested Solicitation

Proxy statement filed in a contested election ΓÇö proxy fights where activist shareholders are opposing management nominees for board seats.

DEFM14A

Definitive Proxy ΓÇô Merger/Acquisition

Definitive proxy statement for a shareholder vote on a proposed merger or acquisition. Contains the full transaction rationale, fairness opinion, pro-forma financials, and voting instructions.

DEFR14A

Revised Definitive Proxy Statement

A corrected or updated version of the definitive proxy after it has already been filed.

DEFS14A

Definitive Proxy ΓÇô Special Meeting

Proxy statement for a special shareholder meeting (not the annual meeting). Special meetings are called for urgent corporate actions: emergency capital raise, emergency M&A vote, charter amendment.

DEF 14C

Information Statement ΓÇô Definitive

Information statement sent to shareholders when a corporate action is approved by written consent of majority holders without a formal meeting vote.

PRE 14A

Preliminary Proxy Statement

Preliminary version of the DEF 14A filed for SEC review before it is sent to shareholders. Required for non-routine matters.

PREC14A

Preliminary Proxy ΓÇô Contested Solicitation

Preliminary proxy in a contested election, filed for SEC review.

PREM14A

Preliminary Proxy ΓÇô Merger

Preliminary merger proxy statement filed for SEC review.

PREN14A

Preliminary Proxy ΓÇô Non-Management

Preliminary proxy filed by non-management parties (activists) for SEC review.

Preliminary version of the information statement (DEF 14C).
