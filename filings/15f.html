General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Foreign private issuer equivalent of 15-12G. Filed to terminate Section 12(g) registration when FPI drops below 300 US shareholders of record.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: Filed when FPI meets eligibility requirements for deregistration.
  • Who: Foreign private issuers exiting SEC reporting under Section 12(g).
