General Information & Instructions

Filing Summary

  • What: Filed when a successor company's securities become registered under Section 12(b) automatically as a result of a merger, reincorporation, or business combination.
  • Why: Tracks the moment a new legal entity officially becomes a public reporting company — used in M&A timeline databases.
  • When: Filed in connection with the succession event.
  • Who: Successor companies in mergers/reincorporations.
