General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Form 2-E is an report of sales pursuant to rule 609 of regulation e.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: This is generally an event-based filing. It must be filed within 4 business days.
  • Who: It must be filed by public entities.

Purpose of 2-E

You may not send a completed printout of this form to the SEC to satisfy a filing obligation. You can only satisfy an SEC filing obligation by submitting the information required by this form to the SEC in electronic format online at https://www.edgarfiling.sec.gov/.
