General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Form 9-M0 is an irrevocable appointment of agent for service of process pleadings and.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: This is generally an event-based filing. It must be filed within 4 business days.
  • Who: It must be filed by public entities.

Purpose of 9-M0

UNITED STATES OMB APPROVAL SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION Not subject to Washington, D.C. 20459 OMB Clearance (44 U.S.C 3501, et. seq.) FORM 9-M IRREVOCABLE APPOINTMENT OF AGENT FOR SERVICE OF PROCESS, PLEADINGS AND OTHER PAPERS BY PARTNERSHIP NON-RESIDENT BROKER OR DEALER THIS FORM SHALL BE FILED IN DUPLICATE ORIGINAL 1.
