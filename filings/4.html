General Information & Instructions

Filing Summary

  • What: Reports every change in an insider's holdings: open-market purchases, open-market sales, option grants and exercises, RSU vesting, gifts, and other transactions. The most frequently filed Section 16 form.
  • Why: The single most-tracked insider signal. Finviz, Yahoo Finance, MarketBeat, and TipRanks all prominently display Form 4 insider buys/sells. Cluster buying (multiple insiders buying simultaneously) is statistically one of the strongest long-term buy signals. Academic studies consistently show insider purchase portfolios outperform.
  • When: Within 2 business days of the transaction.
  • Who: All corporate insiders: officers, directors, 10%+ shareholders.

Understanding the 4 Filing

Reports every change in an insider's holdings: open-market purchases, open-market sales, option grants and exercises, RSU vesting, gifts, and other transactions. The most frequently filed Section 16 form.

By mandating this disclosure, the SEC ensures that financial markets operate with maximum transparency. For stakeholders, analyzing this form removes ambiguity and establishes a reliable factual basis for making informed decisions.

Filing Requirements and Compliance

Ensuring compliance with SEC regulations requires timely and accurate submission of the 4. The mandated timeframe for this form is Within 2 business days of the transaction. . Filing entities—specifically All corporate insiders: officers, directors, 10%+ shareholders. —must adhere rigidly to these deadlines to avoid regulatory penalties, maintain good standing on public exchanges, and preserve investor trust.

Submitting the document typically involves formatting the data via EDGAR (Electronic Data Gathering, Analysis, and Retrieval system), often utilizing standardized XBRL tagging to allow for immediate programmatic analysis by market participants. Delays or material omissions in these disclosures can result in severe repercussions, making a streamlined reporting process essential for modern corporate compliance.
