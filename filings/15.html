General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Suspends the duty to file periodic reports under Section 15(d) when a company has fewer than 300 shareholders and qualifies for suspension.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: Filed when eligibility requirements are met.
  • Who: Companies qualifying to suspend Section 15(d) reporting obligations.
