Schedule 13D

Schedule 13D – Activist Beneficial Ownership

Trigger >5% Ownership
Deadline Within 5 business days of crossing the 5% threshold.
Who Files Any investor or group acquiring 5%+ beneficial ownership with activist intent.
Authority Sec. 13(d) of '34 Act

STRUCTURE

Key Form Components

This SEC filing consists of mandatory disclosures tailored to its specific regulatory purpose. Typical components include:

Item 4

Purpose

The activist's manifesto. Discloses intent to influence strategy, seek board seats, or force a merger/sale of the company.

Item 3

Source of Funds

Reveals if the investor is using their own capital or borrowed funds (margin) to mount an activist campaign.

Item 6

Contracts

Details side agreements, swaps, or hedges that aren't visible on the surface of the share count.

APPLICATION

Regulatory Context

This form serves a distinct function within the SEC's EDGAR database, maintaining market transparency and ensuring fair access to crucial institutional data.

Integrity

Market Integrity

By mandating standardized disclosures, the SEC ensures all market participants have equal access to material information.

Compliance

Legal Compliance

Filers must adhere to strict formatting and timeline rules. Failure to submit or materially misrepresenting facts results in significant enforcement actions.

Signatures

The report must be signed by the registrant and by its authorized officers/directors in accordance with SEC rules.
