Schedule 13G

Schedule 13G – Passive Beneficial Ownership

Trigger >5% Ownership
Classification Passive / QII
Deadline Initial (QIIs/Exempt): within 45 days after quarter-end of crossing 5%. Initial (Passive): 5 business days. Amendments: 45 days after quarter-end of any material change.
Authority Sec. 13(g) of '34 Act
