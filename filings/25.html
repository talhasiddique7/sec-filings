General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Alternative delisting form filed directly by the national securities exchange notifying of a stock's removal from listing.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: Filed when exchange initiates delisting proceedings.
  • Who: National securities exchanges.
