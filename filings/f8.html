General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Registration for securities of Canadian issuers issued in exchange offers or business combinations with other Canadian companies.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: Filed before the exchange offer commences.
  • Who: Canadian issuers in exchange offer transactions.
