General Information & Instructions

Quick Summary

  • What: Annual report filed by foreign governments and their agencies that issue securities in the US (e.g., sovereign bonds). Includes macroeconomic data, debt levels, and government finances.
  • Why: It is required by SEC Mandate to ensure fair disclosure, transparency, and regulatory compliance.
  • When: Typically within 9 months after fiscal year-end.
  • Who: Foreign governments, central banks, and government agencies that have issued securities in US markets.
